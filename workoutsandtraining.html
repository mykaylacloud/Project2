Workouts & Training

Through trial and error I have created a routine that is beneficial for body recompositing. Here are some science-backed workout tips to help you get the most out of your training sessions:

  • Strength Training Basics: What Is Strength Training? It involves lifting weights (barbells, dumbbells, or resistance machines) to make your body physically and mentally stronger.
  • How to Gain Strength? Focus on both muscle size and efficient nervous system activation. Challenge yourself with heavier loads progressively.
  • Consult Your Doctor: Before starting any exercise regimen, talk to your doctor.
  • Monitor Yourself: Pay attention to your body during workouts to avoid trouble.
  • Learn Proper Techniques: Ensure you know the correct way to exercise.
  • Apply the SAID principle: You’ll respond to the specific challenges you give yourself. Consistent practice leads to improvement. Whether it’s lifting weights, doing push-ups, or holding a plank, your body adapts to the demands you place on it.
    • Remember, consistency and gradual progression are key to achieving your fitness goals!
