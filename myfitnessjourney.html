My Journey

Hello, my name is Mykayla Richards. I have been an on again, off again athlete for about 10 years. Welcome to my fitness website. I have played soccer for most of those years. During this time I began my fitness routines, and sometimes losing my way through a dark period in my life. Now I have found a great routine that works for me. It has helped myself and loved ones in my life with my body recompositing. This is why I would like to share my tips, and empower women like me to get in the gym or workout in their homes. Not only to empower them to be active, but feel confident achieving the changes they want for their overall health.
